About

We are one of the fastest growing and reliable firms in the industry for our strength in both traditional as well as innovative construction methods and for our creative, novel approach to cutting-edge technologies and delivery system in industrial and commercial facilities. We've built a completely client-centric business with an emphasis on quality materials, designs, and finish. We're craftsmen that listen to your needs. Incepted in 2015, Ruan Constructions is a cut above the rest. Part of the SK Developers group, an established name in the areas of architecture, construction, and interiors, Ruan Constructions is located in Chennai, a major industrial hub in Tamil Nadu. With an unswerving focus on Design Management, Scheduling, Materials Management, Field Productivity, Equipment Management, Quality Control, Contract Management, Performance Management, Document Management and Safety, we possess in-depth experience and the required expertise to undertake construction of projects of any size involving facilities for the Industrial and Commercial sectors.
